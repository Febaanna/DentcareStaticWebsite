..WHO WE ARE..

DentCare was founded in 1988 by John Kuriakose, a visionary entrepreneur and humanitarian. And now we are the nation's largest dental lab. Based in Muvattupuzha in Kerala, India, DentCare provides solutions to address the evolving needs of the dental care industry across the globe.

We sell world-class dental prostheses products in more than 50 geographies, dutifully serving more than 20,000 entities.

DentCare enjoys ISO 14001:2015, ISO 9001:2015, ISO/IEC 27001:2013 and BS OHSAS 18001:2007 certifications and has CE:1293 marking.

All our products are registered with the US Food and Drug Administration, making them supremely reliable and classy.
