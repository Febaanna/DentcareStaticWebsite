Highlights

DentCare Dental Lab is specialised in the fabrication of dental prostheses. Established in the year 1988 with a vision to fabricate high-quality dental prostheses, DentCare has asserted its stand by setting new international standards for the Indian dental industry. The principal and lone object of DentCare has been to provide supreme quality prostheses to its clinician clients for the onward delight of their patients.

Besides the privilege of being one among dentists' most preferred dental laboratory, DentCare also enjoys numerous other repute and recognition. One element that makes DentCare the cream of the crop is the truth that we offer ample uncomplicated dental solutions at affordable prices without any compromise on client service. DentCare upholds exceptionally positive business ethics and we consider sustained customer base as it highest regarded asset. DentCare is an ISO 14001:2015, ISO 9001:2015, ISO/IEC 27001:2013 and BS OHSAS 18001:2007 certified company in recognition of its quality management processes. DentCare is also registered with US FDA with registration number 3011195066. It is noteworthy that DentCare is the only Indian dental lab with CE marking (Conformité Européenne). DentCare pioneered changes and technological modernization among dental labs in India.

Though we initiated our product portfolio with just white metal crowns, we slowly included PFM crowns, all ceramic crowns/bridges, veneers, inlays, onlays, removable dentures and dental implants to slowly get recognised in the global front. Currently, we provide solutions for almost all dental anomalies.
